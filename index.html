Addition

Addition (+) means finding the total, or sum, by combining two or more numbers.

Exampel: If you have 2 apples and your friend has 3 apples, togehter you have 5 apples which can be writen like this:

2 + 3 = 5

Substraction

Substraction (-) is taking a number away from another.

Exampel: If you have 5 apples and your friend takes 3 apples, you have 2 apples left which can be writen like this:

5 - 3 = 2

Multiplication

The basic idea of multiplication (x) is repeated addition.

Exampel: If you add 5 apples to a bag 3 times, you will have 15 apples in the bag. This can be written using addition:

5 + 5 + 5 = 15

or using multiplication:

5 x 3 = 15.

Division

Division (/ or รท ) is splitting into equal parts or groups.

Exampel: There are 12 apples, and 3 friends want to share them. How many apples should they get each? Answer: They should get 4. This could be written:

12 / 3 = 4
